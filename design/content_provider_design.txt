INTERACTION

URI STRUCTURE

org.whispercomm.shout.provider/<tablename>/<id> can access an individual row in
tablee <tablename> with primary key <id>


DATABASE DESIGN

5 tables in the database
In every table, the primary key column has the name "_ID" in order to maintain
compatibility with automatically populating ListViews.

--User_Shouts--
_ID (PK), Content, Date, Incoming_Shout_ID (FK or null)

--Shout--
_ID (PK), User_ID (FK), Content, Date

--Tag--
_ID (PK), Name

--Tag_Assignment--
_ID (PK), Shout_ID (FK), Tag_ID (FK)

--User--
_ID (PK), Public_Key, Username
